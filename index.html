Dental Wellness

Healthy Teeth Tips

🪥
Brush Gently Twice Daily

Use a soft-bristle toothbrush and avoid aggressive brushing to protect enamel.

🩸
Don't Ignore Bleeding Gums

Early treatment can prevent major dental issues later — visit us if you notice bleeding.

🧃
Reduce Sugary Drinks

Frequent sugar exposure significantly increases cavity risk over time.

📅
Visit Your Dentist Regularly

Routine checkups help detect and treat problems early, before they worsen.

🧵
Clean Between Teeth

Flossing or interdental cleaning significantly improves long-term gum health.
